Mapo Tofu

Mapo Tofu nice and quick

The dish that got me through college. This dish requires very minimal ingredients and preparation.

Ingredients

  • about .25 pounds of Ground Pork
  • 1 block of any tofu chopped into cubes
  • green onions (optional)
  • Soy sauce
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • sesame oil
  • ketchup

Steps! Disclaimer I normally eye ball everything :)

  1. Chop your green onions (if using)
  2. start stir frying pork until the color changes to brown
  3. add about a tbsp of soy sauce to the pork and mix in (depends on how salty your soy sauce is
  4. Mix in the green onions
  5. add tofu to the pan and stir fry for about 5 min
  6. add another tbsp of soy sauce for the tofu to absorb
  7. add a squirt of ketchup or 1 ketchup packet
  8. add a tsp of sesame oil and salt
