Spam Fried Rice

Spam Fried Rice (its getting lazier)

Another dish that got me through college on a budget!

Ingredients

  • half a can of spam
  • white rice
  • two eggs
  • oyester sauce
  • any other veggies you desire but I did not add anything else!

Steps! Disclaimer I normally eye ball everything :)

  1. chop your spam into small pieces or slices
  2. start with frying the spam on a pan
  3. move spam to the side and add eggs to scramble.
  4. once eggs start to take their shape you can mix the spam and eggs together and add your rice
  5. stir fry for a few minutes and add about a tbsp of oyster sauce.
  6. stir fry a few more minutes and you're done!
