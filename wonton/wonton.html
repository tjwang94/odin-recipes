Wonton

Wonton soup made the easy way

The preperation for this recipe is actually very quick! Its the physical folding that takes up the most time.

Ingredients

  • Premade wonton skin (because I'm lazy
  • about 1 pound of ground pork
  • 4-5 green onions chopped very small
  • about a tbsp of minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • sesame oil
  • one egg

Steps! Disclaimer I normally eye ball everything :)

  1. Chop your green onions and Garlic
  2. Put pork in a bowl and mix in about a tbsp of cooking oil of your choice
  3. Add at least 2 tbsp of soy sauce to the pork and mix in (depends on how salty your soy sauce is
  4. Mix in the green onions and garlic
  5. Add in about 1 tbsp of salt
  6. Crack 1 egg and mix in with a little bit of sesame oil
  7. Now fold!
